About Astro Motion

Ever wonder how the universe moves? From the orbit of planets to the spin of stars, astro motion is the cosmic dance that shapes our skies! Earth's rotation gives us day and night, while its orbit around the Sun brings us the changing seasons.

Want something cool? Planets sometimes appear to move backward in the sky – it’s called retrograde motion! And over thousands of years, even the stars seem to shift due to Earth's slow wobble.

Whether you're stargazing or tracking a planet's path, astro motion is always at play. Keep looking up!
